Barney Stinson

Summary

Barney Stinson is one of Ted Mosby's friends. He is witty and cunning with his ways to obtain women. He attempts to be Ted Mosby's wing man. He is known for manipulating women into sleeping with him. He has catchy catchphrases. In addition, he deeply cares for his friends but tends to hide it.
