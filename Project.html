Summary

How I Met Your Mothers is an American sitcom, created by Craig Thomas and Carter bay. The series ran for 2005 to 2014. The show follows the main character, Ted Mosby, and his group of friends in New York. His friends are Marshall Erikson, Barney Stinson, Lily Aldrin, and Robin Scherbatsky. The shows recounts the main characters story of how he met his children's mother.
