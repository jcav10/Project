Marshall Erikson

Summary

Marshall Erikson is Ted Mosby's best friend. He begins the show as a student at law school struggling to handle his relationship with Lily Aldrin and law school. Ted Mosby was his random dorm roommate at college where they became close to one another. He also met Lily Aldrin in college. He is studying to become an enviromentalist.
