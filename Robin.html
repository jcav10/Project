Robin Scherbatsky

Summary

Robin Scherbatsky is one of Ted Mosby's friends. She is a news anchor who recently came from Canada to New York on a business trip. Ted Mosby saw Robin at a bar for the first time. Ted instantly fell in love with Robin. Both Ted and Robin show affection to one another but never acts upon it.
